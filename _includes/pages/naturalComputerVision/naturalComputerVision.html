making sense of visual data

Loenz is a tech startup from Amsterdam, NL, founded by Bart and Arjen in November 2015. We focus on computer vision applied to naturalistic data. In essence, we try to make sense of visual data. Our goal is to help companies benefit from this exciting new technology. Currently, we are working on a cloud based service to collect gender, age and group composition from people visiting shops.
